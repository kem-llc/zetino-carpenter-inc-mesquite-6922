Trim & Molding

Add elegance with crown molding, baseboards, wainscoting, and custom trim.

Overview

Our trim and molding services bring architectural detail to any room. We specialize in crown molding, chair rails, picture frames, and custom wainscoting. Our precise miter cuts and seamless joints create a polished, high-end look that transforms ordinary spaces into extraordinary ones.

What's included

  • Enhances room aesthetics and value
  • Professional seamless installation
  • Wide range of profiles and styles
  • Paint-ready or stained finishes

How it works

1

Design Selection

We help you choose molding profiles and layout that complement your home's style.

2

Precision Cutting & Fitting

Our team cuts each piece with exact measurements for tight, gap-free joints.

3

Finishing Touches

We sand, caulk, and prepare the surface for painting or staining, leaving a flawless finish.
