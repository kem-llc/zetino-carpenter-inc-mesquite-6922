Overview

Expand your living space outdoors with a custom deck or patio from Zetino Carpenter Inc. We design and build decks that complement your home and lifestyle, using pressure-treated lumber, composite materials, or a combination. Our decks include built-in seating, planters, and lighting options for the ultimate outdoor experience.
