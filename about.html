Our story

Crafting Quality Since 2008

To provide Mesquite and surrounding communities with exceptional carpentry services that combine traditional craftsmanship with modern techniques, ensuring every project adds beauty and value to your home.

Zetino Carpenter Inc was founded by Juan Zetino, a master carpenter with over 15 years of experience. Starting as a solo contractor, Juan built a reputation for meticulous work and honest service. What began as small repair jobs for neighbors grew into a full-service carpentry business serving homeowners throughout Mesquite, TX.

Today, our team continues that legacy. We take pride in every cut, joint, and finish, treating each home as if it were our own. Our commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted name in local carpentry.
